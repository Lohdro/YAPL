Back to Event Calendar Young Friends of the Abramson Cancer Center Happy Hour Wine, Beer, & Vodka Tasting Fundraiser Event

Date:
Wednesday, March 14, 2014
Time:
6:00 - 8:00 PM
Address:
R2l
Liberty Two
37th Floor
50 South 16th Street
Philadelphia
Sponsor:
Abramson Cancer Center
Description:

Join the Young Friends of the Abramson Cancer Center for a Wine, Beer, and Vodka Tasting. Enjoy spectacular views of Philadelphia from R2l, network with friends, learn about wine and beer from the experts and hear from young scientists about their innovative research - all while supporting a good cause. Support our young scientists and clinicians. Help them pursue their novel ideas so that cancer ends with this generation!

Tickets are $75 in advance and $90 at the door. Grand Raffle Prize: Two tickets to the March 20th Philadelphia Flyers vs. Dallas Stars hockey game and exclusive behind the scenes Press Tour.

Registration:
Registration information to follow.
Event URL:
http://www.pennmedicine.org/Abramson/YoungFriends

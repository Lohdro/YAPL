Mesquite Specialty Clinic brings nationally-recognized pediatric care close to home for families living in the Mesquite and surrounding areas. Offering extraordinary subspecialty care, outpatient surgery, imaging, as well as physical medicine and rehabilitation, Mesquite Specialty Clinic offers a wealth of resources under one roof.

The doctors, surgeons and nurses at Children’s all have special training in the care of children. Most of them are also trained in a specific pediatric specialty. Affiliated with UT Southwestern Medical Center, our care team establishes Children’s as one of the nation’s premier pediatric hospitals.

Conveniently located on the westbound access road of Highway 114, we are between the Carroll Avenue and White Chapel Boulevard exits. Our modern building is certified as environmentally friendly. It was built with a commitment to your convenience and with your child in mind - complete with a kids play area, family resource center and free parking.

Specialties

Pediatric Subspecialties at Mesquite Specialty Clinic

Cardiology

The cardiology service at Children’s Southlake is part of the larger, nationally renowned Heart Center at Children’s Dallas. The Heart Center offers a full program of specialized care including research, treatment and education for children with congenital and acquired heart diseases. Our specialists work together to meet the full range of pediatric cardiac needs.

Endocrinology

The Endocrinology Center at Children’s offers state-of-the-art diagnoses, treatment and management of diabetes, obesity, growth and other hormone-based conditions. Clinical research studies and trials incorporate cutting-edge technology into your child’s care. The endocrinology service at Children’s Southlake is part of the Endocrinology Center at Children’s Dallas.

ENT

Kids can’t be kids when they’re sick. That’s why the Ear-Nose-Throat (ENT) specialists on the medical staff at Children’s offer state-of-the-art treatment for pediatric head and neck problems including hearing loss, sleep apnea, voice difficulties and other ear-nose-throat (ENT)-related conditions. Many of the services available at Children’s Dallas are also available at Children’s Southlake. Specialists on the medical staff work together to meet all pediatric ENT needs, and to provide the highest quality clinical and surgical care.

Epilepsy

With the first Pediatric Epilepsy Program in the country to be certified through The Joint Commission Disease-Specific Care program, Children’s is nationally recognized for excellence in Epilepsy treatment. Medical staff performs state-of-the-art epilepsy diagnoses, treatment and management of pediatric seizure problems. Epilepsy care provided at Children’s Southlake is an extension of services we offer at Dallas.

Gastroenterology

Services: Pediatric Outpatient Surgery

All kids get tummy aches. But when the problem is something more severe, the team at Children’s has the knowledge and technology to help. The Gastroenterology program at Children’s Southlake is part of the Children’s Medical Center GI service line that treats a variety of intestinal difficulties, as well as problems of the liver, gallbladder and bile ducts. Children with digestive and nutritional problems receive the latest and greatest care.
